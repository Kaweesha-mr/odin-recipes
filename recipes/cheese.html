Basic Cream Cheese Frosting

Delicious cream cheese frosting made using just butter, cream cheese, vanilla, and confectioners' sugar.

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Butter
  • Vanilla
  • Confectioners' sugar

Directions

Step 1

Beat cream cheese, softened butter, and vanilla together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and creamy.

Step 2

Gradually beat in confectioners' sugar until smooth.
